Street Dogs

Stray dogs often work hard to find food, scavenging through trash and begging from people. Their struggle for survival exposes them to dangers like being hit by vehicles. They may also face abuse or neglect from people who are unkind. Despite their efforts, their lives are filled with hardship and risk.

Abandoned Animals

Stray cats and dogs, abandoned or lost, face tough lives on the streets, struggling with hunger and health issues. They often form colonies or packs and can be wary of humans. Help includes feeding programs and humane population control.

Caged Animals

In meat consumption practices, rabbits and chickens are kept in cages with little comfort and are often subjected to torture before being slaughtered. They are killed in a brutal manner, making their lives very difficult and harsh.
